📝 Basic Invoice Commands

Basic Invoice

I @customer #service $amount

Creates invoice for customer with specified service and amount

With Due Date

I @john_smith #web $500 due:30

Invoice due in 30 days from today

With Quantity

I @acme #logo $250 qty:3

3 logo designs at $250 each

With Email

I @peter_smith #consulting $1000 em:peter@company.com

Includes email address in invoice

🎤 Voice Commands

Natural Speech

"Invoice TechCorp for website development 2500 dollars due 15"

Multiple Items

"Invoice ABC Company for business card design 150 dollars and logo design 200 dollars quantity 3"

With Notes
